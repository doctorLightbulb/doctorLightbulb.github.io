ALFRED CUTLER

THE BACKWOODS BOY

This is a heartwarming story about a youth and his family living on a sheep ranch in northern Missouri, USA.

Fourteen-year-old Alfred Cutler is living on the family sheep ranch in the woods during the late nineteenth century. One day, his pa comes home with distressing news—a large pack of wolves has come to the area, spreading fear throughout the rural community of Dunborough. Pa is worried his flock is in danger because he has not finished the new fold yet. Will they be able to protect the family sheep against this four-footed, blood-thirsty band of marauders?



Please Note: All characters and events in this story are completely ficticious. Any similarities between people, either living or dead, and circumstances, either current or past, are purely coincidental.
